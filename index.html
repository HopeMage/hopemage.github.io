HopeMage's Action Plan

Introduction

The purpose of this website is to outline a strategy
for creating new space on the Internet to support civilizational development.

Knowledge is the basic foundation of human society. In order to advance the development of human civilization,
both the quantity and quality of knowledge must improve, especially in relative proportion to wrong information.

Therefore, creating new sources of high quality information to expand human knowledge is required.

Features

Ideas for a new online platform of the above type are described below.

Knowledge Repository

Self-explanatory. A knowledge platform must have a store of knowledge, information, and data.

Structured Data Objects

Knowledge should be formally structured while still easily readable by humans.
This will improve parsing and contextual analysis of information over simple text.

Data Versioning

The history of changes and updates to data objects should be recorded for user attribution.

Traceable Sources

Data provenance and verification are fundamental requirements for the maintenance of high quality knowledge.
Sources of information must be clearly referenced and organized. Information can also be rated by voting users.

Discussion Graph

Communication should be structured in a n-to-n format, rather than in a list or tree.
Entries can have multiple parents and multiple children.

Plan

1. Publish this document.
2. Develop prototype with the above features.
3. Launch prototype online.
4. Invite users.
5. Grow platform based on feedback.
