Hope Plan

Introduction

The purpose of this website is to outline a strategy
for developing and maintaining new space on the Internet
to support positive civilizational development into the future.

Knowledge is the basic foundation of human society.

In order to advance the development of human civilization,
both the quantity and quality of knowledge must improve,
especially in relative proportion to wrong information.

Therefore,
creating new sources of high quality information
to expand human knowledge is required.

Priorities

1. Advance human civilization.

2. Improve the quantity and quality of available information.

3. Grow awareness of the active state of the world.

4. Establish self-funding operations with continuous improvement.

5. Support other programs and networks with similar objectives.

Features

Ideas for a new online platform to meet the above goals are described below.

Knowledge Repository

A knowledge platform must have a store of useful knowledge, information, and data.

Structured Data Objects

Knowledge should be formally structured while still easily readable by humans.
This will improve parsing and contextual analysis of information over simple text.

Traceable Sources

Data provenance and verification are fundamental requirements for the maintenance of high quality knowledge.
Sources of information must be clearly referenced and organized.

Data Object Versioning

Changes and updates to data objects should be recorded for historical context and user attribution.

Category Labels

Organizational tags spanning across data models. For example, a label for « climate »
which could be used to connect related weather events, technologies, people, etc.

Deduplication

Rather than duplicating the same data object multiple times across different channels,
data should be centralized to a singular instance with multiple connecting vectors.

Ratings

Evaluation of data objects can provide statistical context and meta information about the data itself.
Voting format(s) should be carefully designed. More sophistication than simple up and/or down votes would be preferable.

Discussion Graph

Communication should be structured as n-to-n, rather than in a list or tree format.
Like in a mesh, entries should be able to have both multiple parents and children.

Meritocratic Trustworthiness

User permissions should be based on the quality of their contributions.
Positive contributions should be rewarded, while negative contributions should be curtailed.

Sustainability

In order to meet project goals, the platform must have longevity. Therefore it must be sustainable.
Rather than maximizing growth or profits, keeping costs low while
earning sufficient revenue to maintain operations should be the priority.

The platform should be self-funding and not rely on donations.

Creating revenue likely requires some mix of premium memberships and paid advertising.
Attention must be given to ensure that financialization does not compromise the platform's overall mission.

Plan

1. Publish this document.
2. Develop prototype with the features listed above.
3. Launch prototype online.
4. Invite users to experiment with the platform.
5. Grow platform based on user feedback.
