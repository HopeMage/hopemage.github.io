HopeMage's Action Plan

Introduction

The purpose of this website is to outline a strategy
for creating new space on the Internet to support civilizational development.

Knowledge is the basic foundation of human society. In order to advance the development of human civilization,
both the quantity and quality of knowledge must improve, especially in relative proportion to wrong information.

Therefore, creating new sources of high quality information to expand human knowledge is required.

Priorities

1. Increase quantity and quality of available information online.
2. Spread awareness of what is happening around the world.
3. Maintain efforts through self-funding operations.
4. Support related programs and networks.
5. Advance civilization.

Features

Ideas for a new online platform of the above type are described below.

Knowledge Repository

Self-explanatory. A knowledge platform must have a store of knowledge, information, and data.

Structured Data Objects

Knowledge should be formally structured while still easily readable by humans.
This will improve parsing and contextual analysis of information over simple text.

Traceable Sources

Data provenance and verification are fundamental requirements for the maintenance of high quality knowledge.
Sources of information must be clearly referenced and organized.

Data Object Versioning

The history of changes and updates to data objects should be recorded for user attribution.

Category Labels

Organization filters which span across data models. For example, a label for "climate"
which can be used to tag relevant weather events, cities, businesses, people, etc.

Deduplication

Rather than duplicating the same data object multiple times across different instances,
centralize to a singular instance with multiple contact vectors.

Ratings

Evaluation of data objects can provide statistical context and meta information about the data itself.
Voting format(s) should be carefully designed. More sophistication than simple up and/or down votes would be preferable.

Discussion Graph

Communication should be structured in a n-to-n format, rather than in a list or tree.
Entries can have multiple parents and multiple children.

Meritocratic Trustworthiness

User permissions should be based on the quality of their contributions.
Making positive contributions should be rewarded, while negative contributions should be punished.

Sustainability

In order to meet project goals, the platform must have longevity. Therefore it must be sustainable.
Rather than maximizing growth or profits, keeping costs low while maintaining sufficient revenue should be the priority.
The platform should be self-funding and not rely on donations.

Creating revenue likely requires some mix of premium memberships and paid advertising.
Attention must be given to ensure that financialization does not compromise the platform's overall mission.

Plan

1. Publish this document.
2. Develop prototype with the above features.
3. Launch prototype online.
4. Invite users.
5. Grow platform based on feedback.
