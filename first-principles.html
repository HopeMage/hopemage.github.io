Introduction

This page outlines the reasoning which provides
a foundation and motives for the Hope Action Plan.

Motivations

The basic foundations of ethics and morality teach us to care about the well-being of others.
« Well-being » means general health and happiness.
The opposite of well-being is « suffering ».

There is simply too much suffering in the world still.
This general suffering must be reduced in order to improve global well-being.

The overarching hope is to meaningfully improve the overall well-being of the world.
Everything that follows is simply to fulfill that hope.
