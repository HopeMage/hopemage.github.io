Introduction

(What is this page meant to be? A blog post, an essay, a rant?)

On this page I hope to begin outlining the specific reasoning
which underlies and motivates the overall plan described here.

Motivations

The overarching hope is to meaningfully improve the state of the world.
Everything that follows is simply to fulfill that hope.

The basic foundations of ethics and morality teach us to care about the well-being of others.
Well-being means general health and happiness. Suffering is the opposite of well-being.

Quite frankly, there is still too much suffering in the world.
This general suffering must be reduced in order to improve global well-being.

Expansion

Prioritizing the reduction of suffering is sometimes called negative utilitarianism.
Another related philosophy is called effective altruism.

Suffering is not the only concern, but just the opening step.
Despite the current level of development, human civilization still has much unfulfilled potential.
We must look not just to suffering but also far beyond to fulfilling the potential of humanity.
