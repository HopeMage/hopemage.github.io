Motivations

To improve the state of the world in meaningful ways is the overarching hope of this project.
Everything that follows is simply attempting to fulfill that hope.

The basic foundations of ethics and morality teaches us to care about the well-being of others.
Well-being means general health and happiness. Suffering is the opposite of well-being.

Quite frankly, there is still too much suffering in the world.
This general suffering must be reduced in order to improve global well-being.
