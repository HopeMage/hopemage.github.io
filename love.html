Love

There are many forms of love.
Love of family, love of friends, love of country, etc.
Not all forms of love are romantic or physical.

The greatest form of love is arguably the love of an ideal or principle.

Such loves include the love of freedom, the love of virtue, the love of justice,
the love of altruism, the love of kindness, and the love of love itself.

But what is the greatest of all is the love of hope.
