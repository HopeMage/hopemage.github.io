Altruism

Definition / Altruism is the benevolent act of helping others

Humans are social in nature, and each person lives interdependently with their fellows.
Life is better when sharing and mutually assisting others.
The core of altruism is that essential generosity.

Altruism comes in many forms. One version is called effective altruism.

Effective Altruism

The basic idea of « effective altruism » is to do the most good possible.
Effective altruism is therefore essentially a utilitarian philosophy which seeks to maximize good.
But how exactly is « good » defined?
That is the central question that will determine whether effective altruism is actually effective or not.
